Pelican Paisley

Singer, Song Writer and Producer

Writer and Composer behind Speed of Venom(2020)

I am a graduate of films school, at the City Varsity School of Art, and a self taught musician. currently I have relesed two EPs, Speed of Venom (2020) and The Illusion of Choice (2020). My style of music can be decribed as psycadelic alternaive rock, I employ dreamy synththesizers, heavy guitars and highly reverbed vocals. All of the aforementioned factors are inspired by the likes of Pink Floyd< Kanye West< The Beatles, David Bowie, Frank Zappa and many more.
